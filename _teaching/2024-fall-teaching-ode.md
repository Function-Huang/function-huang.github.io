As a Teaching Fellow for the Honours Ordinary Differential Equations course, my responsibilities included strengthening students' understanding of core concepts and problem-solving techniques. The fellowship ran from September 10, 2024, to December 22, 2024.

Key Responsibilities
======
*   **Weekly Tutorials:** Delivered a one-hour tutorial session each week, focusing on practical exercises and methods for solving various types of ODEs.
*   **Individual Support:** Held one-on-one office hours every week to provide personalized guidance, answer questions, and help students work through challenging problems.
