During the spring semester (January 8, 2024, to May 10, 2024), I served as a Teaching Fellow for the Honours Calculus course, where I helped students build a rigorous foundation in fundamental calculus and analysis principles.

Key Responsibilities
======
*   **Led In-depth Tutorials:** Conducted two-hour tutorial lectures each week, covering advanced topics in Calculus and introductory Analysis to supplement the main lectures.
*   **Provided Individual Mentorship:** Hosted a dedicated weekly office hour to offer one-on-one assistance, helping students solidify their understanding and tackle complex assignments.
